About   us

Rajeev Institute of technology, popularly known as RIT was established under the aegis of Rajeev Education Trust in the year 2008 in Hassan. RIT is a reputed institution for the quality of its faculty and the outstanding caliber of students graduating from the institution. RIT offers everything from academic excellence to leadership qualities, a nurturing environment to cutting edge infrastructure to help students to succeed. The college is built on a campus of about 21 acres and is a technical education center.

Rajeev Institute of Technology is one of the well known colleges in the field of technical education. Established in the year 2008, it is a private co-educational technical engineering college. Located in the city of Hassan Karnataka, it was established under the Rajeev Education Trust. It is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belgaum.

The courses conducted here are Bachelors of engineering and Masters in Business Administration. Rajeev Institute of Technology has managed to make a prominent name in the field of technical education in Karnataka and this is because of the quality of education it provides to its students. Rajeev Institute of Technology conducts Bachelor of Engineering programs in six subjects and one Post Graduate course. Students can take admission in undergraduate courses and the subjects are electronics and telecommunications, computer science and engineering, electrical and electronics engineering, information science engineering, mechanical engineering and civil engineering. People can also apply for the only Masters program conducted in this college which is Masters of Business Administration. To get admission in the above mentioned courses, students should have passed 10+2 from a recognized board. The selection is however based on the norms of the university. For the Masters program the applicant should be a graduate from a recognized university.

Courses

Computer Science and Engineering


Computer Science Engineering (CSE) is an engineering discipline that covers several topics related to computation, programming languages, program design, computer hardware and software and integrates several fields of computer science.

Electronics and Communication Engineering


Electronics and Communications Engineering (ECE) involves researching, designing, developing, and testing of electronic equipment used in various systems. Electronics and Communications engineers also conceptualize and oversee the manufacturing of communications and broadcast systems.

Electricals and Electronics Engineering


Electrical & electronics engineering is an integrated branch of engineering. It also includes the concepts of power generation & distribution communication & machine control . This course deals with the technological aspects of electricity, especially the design & application of circuitry & electronic equipment.

Information Science and Engineering


Information Science and Engineering is a discipline which includes theories, standards, methods and innovations of various different domains like mathematics, cognitive science and information theory to solve complex IT problems.

Mechanical Engineering


Mechanical engineering is the study, design, development, construction, and testing of mechanical and thermal sensors and devices, including tools, engines, and machines. Mechanical engineering careers center on creating technologies to meet a wide range of human needs.
